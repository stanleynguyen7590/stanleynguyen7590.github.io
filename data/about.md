This collaborative research initiative brings together the **University of Transport and Communications (UTC)** in Hanoi, Vietnam, and the **University of Aizu (UoA)** in Aizu-Wakamatsu, Japan. Founded on a shared commitment to advancing computing science and engineering, the partnership fosters cross-cultural academic exchange, joint research supervision, and technology transfer.

Led by **Dr. Duy-Anh Nguyen** (UTC) and **Prof. K.N Dang** (UoA), the collaboration spans areas including computer architecture, embedded systems, intelligent transportation, IoT, and machine learning. Together, we mentor graduate students, co-author publications, and develop solutions that bridge academic research with real-world impact.
